Abstract: Fundamental Combinational Mixed-Logic circuits are
derived from equations. The Boolean equations are expressed in
sum of products (OR of ANDS) form following a five step general
procedure. All circuits are simulated maintaining a NAND-NAND
Logic and NOR-NOR Logic restriction and only a select number
are physically constructed using only NAND-NAND Logic.
