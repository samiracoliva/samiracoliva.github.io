Abstract: The project presents the toggle application common to all flip-flops, through the design and simulation of two circuits using D and JK flip-flops. More specifically, the process explores dividing the clock frequency by 2 and the necessary analysis to build D and JK flip-flop circuits that divide by 2 as well as creating a count down ripple counter with control and a delay circuit.

Key LogicWorks Part Numbers Used:
• 74LS74 (D flip-flop)
• 74LS109 (JK flip-flop)
• SN74S163 Signal Generator
